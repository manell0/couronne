Points/Rating

wins: You earn 1% of the opponent's rating, at the same time the opponent loses the corresponding rating. Ex: If the opponent has a rating of 120, you earn 1.2 rating points from him or her .

Loss: You lose 1% of your own rating while the opponent earns the corresponding rating.

Ex: If you have 80 in rating, you lose 0.8 rating points to the opponent.

Average: Won or lost rating divided by the number of matches starting after two matches played. Messy, but that's it!
